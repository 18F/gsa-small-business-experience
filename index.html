We connect small businesses to federal buyers

GSA manages contracts and programs that let small businesses sell products and services to federal agencies.

Benefits of working with the GSA

  • Access to federal buyers
  • Access to contracts up to 20 years long
  • Contracts set aside for certain business categories like:
    • Small businesses
    • Woman-owned businesses
    • Service-disabled veteran-owned businesses
    • Small disadvantaged businesses 8(a)
    • HUBZone businesses
